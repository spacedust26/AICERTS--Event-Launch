The Timeline of Events

Smart Cities: AI and Blockchain Solutions

June 10, 2024

Held in Singapore, the Smart Cities event will showcase how AI and blockchain are transforming urban environments. Key topics include smart infrastructure, sustainable city planning, and blockchain-based governance models, with case studies from leading smart cities around the world.

AI & Blockchain Summit 2024

July 15-16, 2024

Join industry leaders and innovators at the AI & Blockchain Summit 2024 in San Francisco. This event will feature keynote speeches, panel discussions, and networking opportunities focusing on the convergence of AI and blockchain technologies and their transformative impact on various sectors.

Blockchain and AI Expo Global 2024

September 12-13, 2024

Held in London, the Blockchain and AI Expo Global 2024 is a premier event bringing together technology experts, startups, and investors. Attendees will explore the latest advancements, use cases, and future trends of AI and blockchain integration.

Decentralized AI Conference

October 22, 2024

The Decentralized AI Conference in Berlin will delve into how blockchain technology can enhance the transparency, security, and efficiency of AI systems. The event will feature workshops and expert sessions on decentralized AI applications.

AI in Financial Services: Blockchain Solutions

November 5, 2024

Taking place in New York City, this one-day conference will explore how AI and blockchain are revolutionizing the financial services industry. Topics include fraud detection, smart contracts, and AI-driven financial analytics.

AI & Blockchain for Healthcare Innovation

August 20-21, 2024

This event in Boston will focus on the intersection of AI and blockchain in healthcare. Discussions will cover patient data security, AI-powered diagnostics, and blockchain-based health records, with insights from healthcare professionals and tech experts.
